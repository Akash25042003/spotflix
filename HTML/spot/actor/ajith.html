Ajith

Full Name: Ajith Kumar Subramaniam
= Date of Birth: May 1, 1971
Place of Birth: Secunderabad, Andhra Pradesh (now in Telangana), India
Siblings: Ajith has two brothers and two sisters.
Ajith's film journey began in the early 1990s, and after a few struggles, he rose to stardom with several hit films, becoming a household name in Tamil cinema.Ajith is a school dropout, but his passion for acting led him to pursue a career in cinema. Before venturing into films, he worked in various jobs, including as a mechanic and racing driver. He also briefly pursued modeling.
