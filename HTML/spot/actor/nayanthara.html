Nayanthara

Full Name: Diana Mariam Kurian
Stage Name: Nayanthara
Date of Birth: November 18, 1984
Hometown: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Education: She attended school in various parts of India due to her father's work in the Indian Air Force. She completed her Bachelor's degree in English Literature from Marthoma College, Thiruvalla, Kerala.
Nayanthara began her acting career in Malayalam cinema with the film Manassinakkare (2003), opposite actor Jayaram. The film was a commercial success and marked the beginning of her successful journey in cinema.
