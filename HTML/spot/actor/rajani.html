Rajinikanth

Full Name: Shivaji Rao Gaekwad
Date of Birth: December 12, 1950
Place of Birth: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Background: Rajinikanth was born into a middle-class Marathi family. His father was a police officer, and his mother was a housewife. He faced financial struggles during his childhood and worked various odd jobs before pursuing acting.Rajinikanth began his career in the film industry in the 1970s and rose to prominence in Tamil cinema in the late 1970s and 1980s. He is known for his distinctive dialogue delivery, unique body language, and the ability to portray both action and emotional roles.
