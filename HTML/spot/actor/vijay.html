Vijay

Full Name: Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar
Date of Birth: June 22, 1974
Place of Birth: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
Education: He attended Loyola College in Chennai but dropped out to pursue a career in acting.
Vijay started his film career as a child artist in several films directed by his father. His debut as a lead actor came with the 1992 film Naalaiya Theerpu. Over time, he became a household name, especially in Tamil Nadu, due to his dance skills, action sequences, and romantic roles.
