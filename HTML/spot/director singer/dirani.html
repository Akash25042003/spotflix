Anirudh Ravichander

Full Name: Anirudh Ravichander
Date of Birth: October 16, 1990
Hometown: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
Family: Anirudh hails from a family with a rich artistic background. His father Ravi Raghavendra is an actor, and his mother Lakshmi is a dancer. He is also the nephew of the famous Tamil actor Rajinikanth.
Anirudh has worked with top stars in the Tamil film industry, including Rajinikanth, Vijay, Ajith, Dhanush, and Sivakarthikeyan. His ability to adapt his music style to suit the theme of the film and the personality of the actor has made him a favorite among directors and actors alike.
