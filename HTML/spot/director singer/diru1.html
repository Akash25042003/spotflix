Yuvan Shankar Raja

Full Name: Yuvan Shankar Raja
Date of Birth: August 31, 1979
Family: Son of Ilaiyaraaja, brother to Karthik Raja and Bhavatharini, both musicians.
His family has deep roots in music, and Yuvan grew up surrounded by the rich traditions of Indian classical and film music.
Yuvan began his music career in the late 1990s. His debut as a composer was for the film Aravindhan (1997) at the age of 16. However, it was in the early 2000s that he made a significant impact with his unique sound.
