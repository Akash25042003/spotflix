HipHop Tamizha Adhi,

Full Name: Adhithya Ramachandran Venkatapathy
Stage Name: HipHop Tamizha Adhi
Date of Birth: February 20, 1990
Hometown: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India
Adhi pursued his schooling in Coimbatore and later obtained a degree in Electrical Engineering. However, his passion for music led him to pursue a career in the music industry, especially in hip hop. He also earned a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree, but music remained his true calling.

Adhi made his debut as a music director in Tamil cinema with the film Aambala (2015), directed by Sundar C and starring Vishal. His work on the film was well-received, especially the energetic and peppy songs. He continued to compose for successful films.
