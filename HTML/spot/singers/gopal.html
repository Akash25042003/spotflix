Full Name: Shakthisree Gopalan
Profession: Singer, Songwriter, Music Producer
Genres: Pop, Classical, Jazz, Electronic, Indie
Shakthisree grew up in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, and has a background in classical music, having trained in Carnatic music. She was also interested in Western music from a young age and balanced both styles, which contributed to her versatile singing style. She has a strong academic background as well, having completed architecture before fully diving into her music career. Shakthisree has worked with renowned composers like A.R. Rahman, Santhosh Narayanan, and G.V. Prakash Kumar. Her collaborations with A.R. Rahman, in particular, have been significant in establishing her as one of the most unique voices in Indian playback singing.
