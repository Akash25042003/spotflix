Pradeep Kumar

Full Name: Pradeep Kumar
Profession: Playback Singer, Music Composer, Guitarist
Genres: Folk, Melody, Indie, Film Music
Pradeep Kumar began his career in the indie music scene and quickly gained recognition for his work as a singer and composer in Tamil cinema. He has a raw, deep voice that brings an emotional edge to his performances, making his songs stand out, especially in films that explore deep emotional and social themes.Pradeep is known for his minimalistic and rustic style, often focusing on melody and simplicity, which allows his emotive singing to shine.Outside of film music, Pradeep Kumar has been involved in indie projects, where he experiments with different genres and explores new sounds, showcasing his versatility and passion for music.
